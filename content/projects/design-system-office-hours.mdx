## Idea

Shared systems only work when teams can understand them quickly and adapt them with confidence. Office hours became a simple way to reduce friction: open feedback, live pattern reviews, and direct collaboration with engineering.

The ritual improved adoption because it made the design system feel like a helpful product rather than a rulebook.
