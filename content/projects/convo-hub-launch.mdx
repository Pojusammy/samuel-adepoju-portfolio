## Product goal

Convo Hub was built around a simple idea: online communities need better conditions for thoughtful participation. That meant designing not only for activity, but for tone, pacing, and trust.

I led the product from idea to launch, shaping the information architecture, interaction patterns, brand direction, and handoff across web, iOS, and Android.

## What the work involved

- framing the core member journey from onboarding to participation
- simplifying discussion creation and moderation workflows
- defining a design language that felt warm, credible, and scalable
- collaborating closely with engineering to translate product intent into a shippable system

## Reflection

The most valuable part of the project was designing for relational clarity. The UI had to communicate not just what people could do, but what kind of space they were entering and how it would feel to participate there.
