## Why I explored this

A lot of portfolio writing either collapses into vague storytelling or overwhelms readers with process trivia. I wanted a middle ground: a format that lets strategic thinking, product constraints, and visual craft all coexist.

The layout experiments borrowed from premium editorial blogs where long-form reading feels deliberate and breathable.
