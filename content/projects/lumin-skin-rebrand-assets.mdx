## Scope

This work sat at the intersection of brand and product. The goal was to elevate the digital experience without losing the brand’s clarity or drifting into cosmetic excess.

I designed supporting assets for the website rebrand that helped the broader experience feel more refined, cohesive, and premium.

## Design principle

The best outcome here was subtle: cleaner pacing, stronger visual consistency, and an overall sense that the brand had matured without becoming complicated.
