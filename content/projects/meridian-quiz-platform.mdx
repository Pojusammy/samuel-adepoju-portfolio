## Business context

Meridian needed a more effective way to guide customers toward products that matched their needs. The existing experience assumed too much confidence at the start, which meant hesitation and drop-off.

The quiz platform created a more supportive path. It asked clearer questions, framed recommendations with better explanation, and reduced the cognitive load of deciding.

<Callout title="Result">
  The initiative drove a 10% conversion rate and contributed more than $5M in business value by helping users make quicker, more confident decisions.
</Callout>

## Design notes

I kept the interface visually restrained so the language and recommendation logic could do the heavy lifting. The real design work was in sequencing questions, calibrating reassurance, and helping the recommendation feel earned rather than arbitrary.
